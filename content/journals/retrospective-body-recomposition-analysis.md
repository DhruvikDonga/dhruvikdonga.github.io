## Observation  
---
Following a job transition and prolonged stress exposure (approx. 3 years), multiple physical and behavioral regressions were observed:
- Poor mind body connection
- Restlessness and laziness
- Poor body shape and underconfidence
- Overweight 89.9kgs 
- Poor diet 

## Experiment  
---
The experiment focused on environmental restriction + nutritional precision + structured resistance training.

- New company and less job stress compare to previous 
- PG group circle was not there so overall social gathering in weekends is reduced based on 2 points.
- Better chance for sleep
- Already habitual for early rise at 5 am
- No friends or family or interuption
- Good trainer

I had my gym training every morning from 6am to 7:30am . Plan was Body Recomposition that is weight loss and muscle training as well ,and it had different phases 

### 1st Month (May) :-
- 4 days Weight training and 2 days Cardio
- Mandatory sleep at 10 am
- No sugar and close to 0 carbs intake
- Heavy protein and fat rich food
- Took no leaves in Gym at that month
- Cancelled friend trip

### 2nd Month (June):-
- 4 days Weight training and 2 days Cardio
- Mandatory sleep at 10 am
- No sugar and close to 0 carbs intake
- Heavy protein and fat rich food
- Took no leaves in Gym at that month

### 3rd Month (July):-
- 5 days Weight training and 1 day Cardio
- Mandatory sleep at 10 am
- No sugar and close to 0 carbs intake
- Heavy protein and fat rich food
- Took 1 sick leave in Gym at that month

Focus was to stick to one routine and not break it in any worse situation and avoid it as much as possible .

## What changed in my thinking  
---
### Initial Assumptions:-

- Carbs rich food is healthy and only option
- Gym trainer won't help much
- You can do body recomposition while being socially active in weekends
- Doing cardio is only enough and long term solution

### Revised Conclusions After 12 Weeks:- 

- Cut down Carbs and sugars and replace them with proteins , fats rich food 
- Take social activeness close to 0 in 3 months phase or to least possible value
- Stick to single pattern of activites for 3 months so that brain and body balance can happen properly else it can break even in if a random old activitiy like craving food or not going gym is done 1-2 times . Cause recovering will be tough from there
- Control and track sleep
- Workout is Gym - Diet - Recovery , so it should be consistent and predicable and no in between disruption like cheat meal , late night weekend sleep , gym skip.
- Invest on trainer even it looks expensive but its investment and treat it that way only
- Focus on weight training more and more and try to reduce cardio as goal is to increase Base metabolism rate of the body rather then only weight loss
